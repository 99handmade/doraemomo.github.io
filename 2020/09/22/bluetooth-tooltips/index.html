A simple interpretation of RSSI value

An imprecise but well known interpretation:

RSSI (dBm) interpretation
>= -30 dBm amazing
>= -67 dBm very good
>= -70 dBm okey
>= -80 dBm not good
>= -90 dBm unusable
< -90 dBm unreachable
